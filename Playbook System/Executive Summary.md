# Executive Summary - Governance Playbooks

---

## 1. Purpose of the Playbooks

The AUMI Governance Playbooks translate high-level Responsible AI principles into actionable operational controls.

They define how organisations should detect, respond to, resolve, and prevent failures in AI systems.

Together, the playbooks form the backbone of AUMI’s operational governance model.

---

## 2. Role in the AUMI Framework

The playbooks operate within the AUMI lifecycle:

Policy → Risk → Evaluation → Monitoring → Incident → RCA → Improvement → Policy Update

They govern the Monitoring, Incident, and Recovery phases.

---

## 3. Playbook Portfolio Overview

### Playbook 1 - Model Performance Degradation

Focus: Quality, reliability, and user trust

Controls:

* Accuracy monitoring
* Root cause diagnostics
* Rollback procedures
* Validation protocols

---

### Playbook 2 - Data Quality Incident

Focus: Data integrity and lineage

Controls:

* Pipeline monitoring
* Embedding validation
* Schema governance
* Reprocessing workflows

---

### Playbook 3 - Hallucination & Safety Incident

Focus: Harm prevention and compliance

Controls:

* Safety containment
* Policy enforcement
* Adversarial testing
* Human review mechanisms

---

### Playbook 4 - Monitoring Alerts Response

Focus: System observability and stability

Controls:

* Alert triage
* Impact assessment
* Infrastructure failover
* Cost governance

---

### Playbook 5 - Prompt Failure & Regression

Focus: Change management and instruction integrity

Controls:

* Version control
* Regression testing
* Approval workflows
* Emergency rollback

---

## 4. Integrated Governance Value

Collectively, the playbooks provide:

* End-to-end incident management
* Risk-based escalation
* Executive accountability
* Regulatory alignment
* Continuous improvement loops

They enable organisations to move from ad-hoc AI operations to institutional-grade governance.

---

## 5. Executive Benefits

For leadership and oversight bodies, the playbooks:

* Reduce operational risk
* Improve regulatory readiness
* Increase system reliability
* Strengthen public trust
* Support responsible innovation

They provide visibility and control over complex AI systems.

---

## 6. Adoption Guidance

Organisations adopting AUMI should:

1. Assign governance roles
2. Map playbooks to business systems
3. Integrate with existing risk programs
4. Train operational teams
5. Establish review cadence

Phased adoption is recommended.

---

## 7. Conclusion

The AUMI Governance Playbooks represent a mature, practical approach to managing AI risks in real-world environments.

They enable sustainable, ethical, and resilient deployment of AI systems at scale.
